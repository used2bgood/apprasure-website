Why Appraisal?

Differences in estimates are inevitable.

Adjusters and contractors often interpret damage, scope, and repair methods differently. They may disagree on what’s affected, what it takes to fix it, or what it should cost — not out of bias, but because they approach the problem from different angles.



Every project is unique.

Materials, installation methods, and workmanship standards can vary. While estimating software helps, two professionals may still arrive at different conclusions.



AppraSure’s Role.

We provide a structured, fair, and neutral appraisal process. Our experience ensures disputes are resolved with clarity and professionalism.
