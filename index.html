How AppraSure Helps

Every Project is Unique


No two claims are alike. Age, materials, installation methods, and local codes all impact the scope and cost of repairs. We approach each property with a fresh, tailored evaluation — never a cookie-cutter estimate.

Why Estimates Differ


Even with standardized estimating tools, two qualified professionals may reach different conclusions. Adjusters often focus on policy and pricing, while contractors consider practical build methods. Both perspectives matter — and both need to be reconciled.

When to Consider Appraisal


  • The adjuster’s estimate is lower than the contractor’s
  • Disagreement on materials or repair methods
  • Concerns over compliance with building codes
  • Deadlock that risks delaying needed repairs

AppraSure Bridges the Gap


Disagreements over scope, method, and cost are common. We help find common ground through evidence-based, policy-compliant solutions. AppraSure combines hands-on construction expertise with in-depth estimating knowledge, giving us a unique perspective that few others can offer. This dual experience allows us to understand both the practical realities of the job site and the technical details of the estimate, enabling us to find common ground more effectively.

Our Services

  • Claim Appraisal – Appointed as a neutral to resolve scope/value disputes.
  • Scope Review – We review estimates from both sides and provide a neutral analysis.
  • Remote Appraisal – Virtual inspections via photos, video, or Matterport.
